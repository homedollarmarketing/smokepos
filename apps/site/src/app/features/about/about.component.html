Our Story

Mr. P Authentic Autoparts was founded with a singular vision: to bring trusted, authentic, and high-performance parts to the African automotive market. We recognized that owners of luxury European vehicles deserved access to the same quality parts available anywhere in the world.

Today, we stand as Uganda's premier destination for genuine auto parts, serving discerning customers who refuse to compromise on quality. From Range Rover to Mercedes-Benz, BMW to Audi — we deliver the same excellence their engineers intended.

Our commitment to authenticity isn't just a promise — it's the foundation of everything we do. Every part we sell is verified, every customer we serve is valued, and every vehicle we touch receives our best.
